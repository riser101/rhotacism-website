The mission

This is not a widget. This is a clinical tool that changes lives.

Our founder grew up with rhotacism — the inability to produce the 'R' sound — and spent years navigating expensive, inconsistent speech therapy sessions that were hard to access and harder to afford. That experience is the engine behind everything we build. TopSpeech exists because that story shouldn't be as common as it is.

Today we run three programmes—Rollr Academy for rhotacism, Stutter Academy for fluency, and Lisp Academy for sibilant sounds—each designed to deliver clinically grounded, state-of-the-art articulation help that used to require a specialist, a waiting list, and a significant bill. We’re changing that equation, one user at a time.

How we work

The AI Sandwich method.

We don't treat AI as magic and we don't treat it as a threat. We've developed a working method that keeps human judgment at the centre while letting AI do what it does best: explore the possibility space at speed.

The AI Sandwich
1
Human intuition first

Define the real problem. What's broken, why it matters, what constraints actually exist.

2
AI exploration

Blast the problem space. Generate, combine, discard, compare. Use every model and every tool at your disposal.

3
Human judgment closes it

You decide. You filter the AI's output through taste, context, and strategic sense. You take responsibility. Then you ship it.
