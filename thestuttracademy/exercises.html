    "Growling" Method

    Make gentle growling sound and gradually quiet it to R sound

    Instructions

    4. Practice for full duration

    Tongue Positioning

    Practice proper tongue placement for R sound

    Instructions
    1. Place tongue tip behind lower teeth
    2. Raise back of tongue toward soft palate
    3. Create narrow channel for air flow
    4. Sustain R sound for 10 seconds
    5. Repeat 5 times
    Tips
    • Use a mirror for visual feedback
    • Don't let tongue tip touch roof of mouth
    • Keep sides of tongue touching upper molars 🦷

    Acoustic Interpretation Guide

    This tool maps vocal tract resonance (Formants). Adjusting tongue shape moves the frequency peaks in real-time.

    The F3 Formant

    The 3rd Formant (F3) is the primary diagnostic marker for the /r/ phoneme. When tongue position is correct, F3 drops toward the target frequency range.
