Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 15, 2024

Your privacy is important to us. The Rollr Academy is designed to protect your personal information by keeping everything on your device.

What We Do NOT Collect or Store

We do not store any personal data on our servers. Specifically:

  • Audio recordings stay on your device only
  • No usage patterns or interaction data is collected
  • No personal information is stored on our servers
  • No speech analysis data is retained by us

What Information We Do Collect

The only information we collect is:

  • Email addresses: When you voluntarily sign up for early access updates
  • Basic website analytics: Anonymous visitor statistics via Google Analytics

How Your Audio Works

When you use our speech assessment:

  • Audio is recorded directly in your browser
  • Processing happens on external AI services temporarily
  • Results are returned to your device
  • No audio files are stored anywhere permanently

Third-Party Services

We use minimal third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: For anonymous website usage statistics
  • Google Cloud: For temporary audio processing (data not stored)
  • Email service: For sending updates (only if you subscribe)

Your Data, Your Control

Since we don't store your personal data:

  • Your audio recordings remain private on your device
  • You can clear your browser data anytime
  • No data deletion requests needed (we don't have your data)
  • Unsubscribe from emails anytime

Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted here with a new effective date.
