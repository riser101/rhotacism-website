About

Peter Stoll is a dedicated speech-language pathologist with expertise in the evaluation and treatment of communication disorders. Based in Rockville, Maryland, Peter brings a strong clinical foundation and commitment to evidence-based practice in speech-language pathology.

As an ASHA-certified clinician, Peter maintains the highest standards of professional practice and continues to advance his expertise in speech sound disorders and therapeutic interventions.

Clinical Expertise

  • Evaluation and treatment of speech sound disorders
  • Evidence-based therapy approaches for articulation disorders
  • R sound production and rhotacism remediation
  • Communication development across the lifespan
  • Speech-language pathology assessment and intervention

Education & Credentials

Peter earned his Master of Science degree in Speech-Language Pathology from Long Island University Brooklyn (2014-2017). He holds the Certificate of Clinical Competence in Speech-Language Pathology (CCC-SLP) from the American Speech-Language-Hearing Association (ASHA).

Contributions to The Rollr Academy

At The Rollr Academy, Peter contributes his clinical expertise to help develop and refine therapeutic protocols for our speech therapy programs. His background in speech-language pathology ensures that our treatment approaches maintain clinical rigor and effectiveness.

Peter's dedication to evidence-based practice helps translate proven therapeutic methods into accessible exercises that users can practice at their own pace while achieving meaningful results.
