Frequently Asked Questions

Is this program actually for adults/teens, or is it just for children? +
You are looking at a program built for adults. While many programs focus on children, Stutter Fluency Centre is specifically engineered for the adult and teen brain. We focus on "Finding Your Flow" in the high-stakes environments you face now: job interviews, presentations at work, and social situations.
Will I have to talk to a stranger? I’m nervous about "face-to-face" therapy. +
We understand that traditional therapy offices can feel like a spotlight. Our program is 100% private and self-paced. You record your speech samples in the comfort of your own home. Our algorithm and clinical team analyze your data behind the scenes, giving you the feedback you need without the social anxiety of a waiting room.
I’m terrified of being seen or judged. How private is this? +
100% private. We understand that the fear of judgment is a core component of stuttering. There are no face-to-face appointments, no waiting rooms, and no uncomfortable eye contact while you struggle with a word. All your recordings—from your initial assessment to your biweekly check-ins—are stored securely in your private portal. You share your progress with the algorithm and your own eyes only (unless you choose to share it with an SLP).
How is this different from watching YouTube videos on speech therapy? +
Content is not coaching. YouTube can't listen to you; we can. Stutter Fluency Centre provides Biweekly Progress Tracking. We analyze your specific recordings, compare them to previous weeks, and adjust your personalized program based on your actual data. It’s the difference between watching a workout video and having a personal trainer.
I’m a student/working professional. How much time does this take daily? +
We designed this for busy lives. All it takes is 15 minutes of daily practice. Because the program is accessible "Anytime," you can do your exercises during a lunch break, on your commute, or right before bed. Consistency beats intensity every time.
Do I need any special equipment or apps to use this? +
All you need is your smartphone, tablet, or computer with a microphone (which almost all modern devices have). The platform works right in our secure app. You record audio directly on the device you already use every day. No need to buy headsets or download complicated software.
What exactly am I paying $249 for? +
You are getting a full year's worth of clinical-grade therapy (typically costing $1,500–$3,000) for a fraction of the price. This includes your personalized 16-week curriculum, biweekly AI-driven speech analysis, and lifetime access to our fluency toolset. No hidden per-session fees.
Can I try it before I commit to the full cost? +
Yes. We offer a 7-day free trial. You can dive into the first week of modules, start your daily practice, and see the interface for yourself. If it doesn't feel like the right fit for your voice, you can cancel anytime—no questions asked.
What happens after the 12 or 16 weeks? Will I lose my progress if I stop practicing? +
The goal is to make fluency automatic so it becomes your default setting. In the final weeks of the program ("Real-World Application"), you transition from structured exercises to spontaneous conversation. Once the program ends, you own the skills. However, you retain access to the materials and your progress tracker for reference. If you ever feel a "rustiness," you can simply revisit a specific week's module for a refresher—it’s your toolkit for life.
