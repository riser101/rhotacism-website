OUR STORY

Born from Personal Experience

Why We Started The Rollr Academy

The Rollr Academy was founded by individuals who personally struggled with rhotacism into adulthood. We understand the frustration, embarrassment, and impact it has on professional and personal confidence.

Traditional speech therapy, while effective, often requires expensive appointments, long waiting lists, and the discomfort of practicing in front of others. We knew there had to be a better way.

Combining evidence-based speech therapy techniques with modern technology, we created a solution that's accessible, private, and effective for busy adults worldwide.

Our Mission

Making R Sound Correction Accessible

Our mission is simple: make effective rhotacism treatment accessible to every adult who needs it, regardless of location, schedule, or budget.

We believe that clear speech is a fundamental right, not a privilege. Everyone deserves to speak with confidence without being limited by geographical barriers or financial constraints.

Through our program, we're democratizing access to professional-quality speech therapy techniques, bringing them directly to your smartphone.
