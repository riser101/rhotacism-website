Make gentle growling sound and gradually quiet it to R sound

Instructions

  2. Gradually make it quieter
  3. Transform it into an R sound
  4. Practice for full duration
Tips
  • Start with a relaxed throat
  • Don't force the sound

Tongue Positioning

Practice proper tongue placement for R sound

Instructions
  1. Place tongue tip behind lower teeth
  2. Raise back of tongue toward soft palate
  3. Create narrow channel for air flow
  4. Sustain R sound for 10 seconds
  5. Repeat 5 times
Tips
  • Use a mirror for visual feedback
  • Don't let tongue tip touch roof of mouth

How to Use the Spectrogram

Reading the Spectrogram

The wave shows your voice frequencies. Look for 3 peaks (bumps).

The 3rd Peak is Key

  • Correct R: 3rd peak close to the orange target line
  • "eee" sound: peaks spread apart
  • "ahh" sound: peaks close together

Tips

  • Use earbuds with mic for best results
  • Make a sustained "grrrr" sound
  • Watch the 3rd peak move as you adjust
